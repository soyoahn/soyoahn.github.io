## Universal Studios Hollywood with toddlers

> We're taking a spontaneous trip with our 5yo (41") and 3yo (36"). 

Important: This is less of a blog post and more like a login-free thing I can share with my party so we can all refer to it on the day-of. This is what we did for our [Disneyland trip](../../01/26/disneyland.html) and it was pretty helpful throughout the day. 

Hours on our day: **9am-8pm**

--- 

### <font color="#FF5003">Lower Lot</font>
* **Mario Land character meet & greets**
* 🍄 Toadstool Cafe (11am) - Reserve In Advance
* Note: We didn't end up getting a Power-Up band and my kids were thankfully okay about it--Next time, we'll borrow one! 

### <font color="#FF5003">Upper Lot</font>
* **Waterworld Show - Check Showtimes Day-Of**
* **💦🩳 Super Silly Fun Land** - Bring water shoes, swimwear for the kids, quick-dry towel
* <s>Jurassic World - 42" min; let's see if they'll let our 5yo in</s> We didn't end up attempting this
* <s>Kung Fu Panda</s> We didn't end up attempting this
* Studio Tour - 1hr - Use the bathroom just before

### <font color="#FC9020">Reminders</font>
* Take kids potty before standing in a long line, and check at least every 2 hours
* Keep kids hydrated so they stay feeling good
* Change kids into PJs at some point so they can sleep on the drive home.
* Misc packing list: 
  * Water bottles for everyone
  * Portable sound soother
  * Snacks (3 small apples, 2 bananas, goldfish, made good granola bites)
  * Wet wipes (for quick cleanup after snacks and meals)
  * Quick dry towel, water shoes for after Super Silly Fun Land
  * Change of clothes for after Super Silly Fun Land
  * Power bank(s)
  * For the car: Toothbrushes, toothpaste, PJs

### <font color="#853DEE">Brief Plan</font>
Example: We picked a mid-week school day. 

✅ Check [ride heights](https://www.universalstudioshollywood.com/web/en/us/ride-height-requirements) in advance for kiddos. <br>
✅ Get familiar with the app & map far in advance of the trip (wait times, dining reservations, mobile ordering, events, and so on). <br>
✅ Pack water shoes (required for Super Silly Fun Land) and a change of clothes for kiddos

Post-Mortem: Super Silly Fun Land, the Waterworld show, and the Studio Tour were the attractions our two toddlers enjoyed the most!
